Celebrating our Physician & Practitioner Award winners

Our inaugural Meridian Physician & Practitioner award winners come from unique backgrounds and organizations. What they share is a measurable commitment to excellence, as evidenced by their HEDIS® performance. These honorees ensure members receive timely access to care, necessary follow-up appointments, screenings, immunizations, and more.

“Our providers are essential partners in providing quality care, and these awards recognize their dedication to providing the best care,” says Meridian Plan President and CEO Cristal Gary. “Each recipient has made caring for people’s health a top priority and continued to move toward improving health equity.”

Earn incentives by screening members for social determinants of health

To promote whole-person care, contracted Meridian Medicaid and YouthCare providers can earn incentives when they screen members for social determinants of health (SDoH) and add Z-codes to claims. Z-codes are ICD-10-CM diagnosis codes used to document SDoH data (e.g., housing, food insecurity, transportation, etc.). Learn how to take advantage of this program, which runs through Dec‍ember 3‍1.

Stay vigilant in monitoring potential measles exposure

With confirmed measles cases recently reaching their highest levels in over 30 years, elevated risk of exposure to the disease—especially during the summer travel season—makes it important to stay informed. The Department of Healthcare and Family Services (HFS) recently shared updated guidance regarding coverage of the measles, mumps, and rubella (MMR) vaccine, outlining when an early or extra dose of the MMR vaccine may be appropriate for infants.

Have new staff or need a refresher?

Our Provider Engagement team hosts regularly scheduled training opportunities to support providers.
